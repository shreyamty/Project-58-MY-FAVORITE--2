My Favorite Hobby
Reading Books

One of my hobbies is reading fictional books. Whenever I have some free time, I like to use some of it to read. I am 10-years-old and I am going to 5th grade when school will start again after summer break. I know that reading is a good hobby to have, since it developes creativity and brings one to a completely different world. One can also learn some important life lessons from books.
